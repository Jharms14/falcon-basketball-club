Welcome to the 2025-2026 Season - and another year of Youth Falcon Basktball Club

We started this club for one reason: to get kids and our community excited about basketball again. Our high-school programs have had success, but participation at younger ages has slipped. Nationally, regular team-sport participation for kids 6‚Äì12 fell from 45% in 2008 to 38% in 2018. Many kids now quit organized sports by age 11.

Research shows that most kids leave sports not because they aren‚Äôt necessarily good at it, but because the experience stops being fun. They don‚Äôt get enough playing time, the coaching gets too focused on winning, or the season becomes too long and intense. When the joy goes out, so does the participation. With the Falcon Basketball Club we aim to keep the fun, give every kid a role, coach respectfully, and ensure basketball remains a positive part of life‚Äînot a burden.

When kids don‚Äôt belong to something bigger than themselves, they miss reps in the habits life will demand later: work ethic, discipline, teamwork, resilience, confidence, pride, loyalty, and purpose. Sports gave me all of that. And I want to give that back! Coaches and teammates gave me structure and a place to belong. They taught me how to handle adversity and success, learnng that failure is an important learning tool in life, how to compete the right way, and to align effort with faith and purpose.

That‚Äôs why I coach. I love this game and what it gives back. I love our kids and our community and most importantly - I want every kid here to feel they matter when they step outside their comfort zone and choose to be part of a team.
